How EcoGuardian Works

Collaborative Platform for Environmental Projects

Provides a centralized platform for individuals to collaborate on environmental initiatives.

Enables users to initiate and join projects related to cleaning, plantation, and other environmental causes.

Support for Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Aims to complement the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan by addressing the shortcomings of existing cleanliness reporting systems.

Plans to integrate the platform with Swachh Bharat Abhiyan for seamless reporting and addressing of cleanliness issues nationwide.

Educational Resources and Workshops

Offers educational resources to raise awareness about environmental issues.

Conducts workshops in collaboration with NGOs and activists to empower users with knowledge and skills.

Robust Cleanliness Reporting System:

Implements a robust reporting system for cleanliness issues such as littering and lack of infrastructure.

Features a leaderboard to incentivize active participation, with rewards for top contributors.

Verification Mechanism for Reported Issues:

Utilizes AI and ML technologies to verify reported issues and prevent fake or repetitive reporting.

Engages users in the verification process to ensure the authenticity of reported issues.

Funding and Partnerships:

Relies on crowdfunding to sustain and expand platform activities.

Seeks partnerships with NGOs, government agencies, and environmentally conscious companies for funding and support.

Offers sponsorship opportunities to companies in exchange for support and promotion on the platform.
