Our Story: From Assignment to Action
As the leader of EcoGuardian, I witnessed a glaring gap while working on an assignment about Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. The reporting system for cleanliness was inactive, and there was no platform for those eager to improve their surroundings. Inspired by the enthusiasm of NIT Patna students during a Gandhi Jayanti cleaning drive, I founded EcoGuardian to provide a space where individuals can unite for a cleaner, greener India. Our mission extends beyond volunteering; we offer educational resources to empower users in environmental conservation. Join us in turning passion into action!
